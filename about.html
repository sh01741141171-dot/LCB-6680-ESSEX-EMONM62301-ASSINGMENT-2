About Bangladesh

Bangladesh is country in South asia known for its rich culture, traditions, and hospitality.

Language

The main language spoken in Bangladesh is Bangla (Bengali).

Traditional Values

  • Respect for elders
  • Family bonding
  • Kindness and hospitality
  • Strong community support

Popular Festivals

  • Pohela Boishakh (Bengali New Year)
  • Eid-ul-Fitr
  • Eid-ul-Adha
  • Language Movement Day (21 February)
