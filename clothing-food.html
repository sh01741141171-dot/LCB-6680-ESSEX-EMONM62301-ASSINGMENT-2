Traditional Clothing

Women

Women in Bangladesh traditionally wear saree and Salwar kameez.

Salwar kameez

Salwar kameez is also a very popular traditional dress for women in Bangladesh.

Men

Men often wear panjabi especially on culture and religious occasions.
